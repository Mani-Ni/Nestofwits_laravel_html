Features

.

Student Management

From admit cards to transfer certificates, birthday reminders, contact details, etc of all the students in the campus can be stored and viewed at any given time.

Classroom Management

Create and manage classes as per the structure of your institution. This helps to view all the basic information related to a class such as the equipment available, sections, courses related to it and students allotted.

Fee Management

With a faster invoicing process and an integrated financial management suite, an uncomplicated management of every student fee is ensured. This also gives updates related to dues or payment of late fees by a student.

Teaching Management

To ensure a systematic approach in teaching methods by allotting and arranging portions in prior. This lets you create job tables for teachers which gives them an update on the status of their teaching for eg: portions to cover, activities to complete.

Attendance Management

This is an easy to use attendance management feature that lets you track attendance of students and staff subject wise or day wise. Attendance can be calculated by using devices such as RFID/Biometric. You can auto calculate attendance date, generate auto alerts for regular absentees and share the data with students and parents.

Learning Management

An one-stop place to view the complete details of a course beginning from topics, reference books, time-table, discussion forums, blogs, podcasts, etc. This serves as a platform for a robust engagement between the teacher and student.

Timetable Management

Create schedules for different sections of a class or courses. The time-table can be quickly edited by the drag and drop feature. Once created, you can publish the schedule in students app. This pre-setting of the timetable allows allocation of subjects to teachers which helps them to plan in advance.

Lesson Management

In just a few clicks, teachers can plan their lessons well in advance and give feedback in that respective lesson. This ensures a systematic approach.

Staff Management

Manage all your types of employees effectively using our Staff Management Portal. This includes all aspects of an employees such as attendance status, performance review, leave management, payroll tracking, timesheet management and many other activities..

Alumni Management

Alumni management portal keeps track of all the students & teachers who have studied or worked in the institution. News and announcements can be announced through this portal.

User Management & Admin Control Panel

Enables the institution’s administrator to give access permissions to Principal, Vice Principal and other staff for better management and control.

Online Registration

Use this simple registration process to enable a quick and efficient enrollment. You can:

  • Track the sales of your institution’s prospectus.
  • View the results of entrance exams.
  • Fee receipts.
  • Record students’ admission.

Students’ Admission

The process involved in a student’s admission into your institute is simplified by this feature. This can be adapted to the needs of your institution such as registration, allotment of batches according to subjects chosen, advance fee submission and submission of documents. The forms will be available both online and offline. It is initiated to each candidate via template emails.

Mobile app

All updates and announcements related to students and institution will be at your fingertips while using this mobile app. Real time information, collaboration with parents & teachers, notification about events, fees, exam dates, etc are few of the key features. This is available for both android and iOS users.

Hostel

Ease of allocating rooms, tracking hostel fee, managing canteen services can be done using our Hostel Management portal. This provides complete information to the warden from the student profiles, allotment of rooms, room change requests and payment of hostel fees.

News

Any information related to your organisation such as events, culturals, competitions, information to parents, holidays can be published in this portal for parents, students and staff to view anytime.

Report Card

Based on the institution’s educational board regulations, report cards such as assessment register, student mark card, etc can be generated easily with the help of teacher’s comprehensive and regular inputs of the student’s assessment.

Student Profiling

This feature allows you to have a complete profile of a student with automated comments and smooth progression of a student.

Parent/Student Portal

A customised sign-in for parents and students provides transparent information about academic activities. Notification and updates will be sent to parents on their child’s progress, students can access their assignments and participate in various forums. This can be viewed online from any location.

Visitor

The difficult task of managing entry of visitors is now made simple with NOW. You can update and view the visitors information at any time. The visitor can also inform in advance to enter the campus.

Online Exams

The features lets you conduct online exams with ease. Some of the key features include fill or view the students’ information attending the exam, addition/removal of students, selected subject exams, instant result declaration.

Infirmary

The infirmary management module lets you keep track of medical records of every student and their special needs if any. This gives the doctor a history or background of the student when they visit or get admitted at the infirmary.

Offline Exams

The difficult task of managing entry of visitors is now made simple with NOW. You can update and view the visitors information at any time. The visitor can also inform in advance to enter the campus.

GPRS/GPS enabled Transportation

This helps you to manage the transportation of your institution easily. Allows you to track routes, generate SMS alerts, real-time updates on the bus routes and pick up & drop points. This can be enabled in parent-student portal or in staff-student portal as well.

Placement

Placement management lets you have a complete detailing of the placements offered to your students, maintain recruiters’ profile, track status and update announcements. Placement related events and results can be notified to students through this portal.

Recruitment

Recruiting your staff is just easier with our Recruitment management portal. The forms are available online and guarantees all the information of an employee in a single place.

Inventory and Purchase

Fully fledged inventory and purchase management portal that helps in keeping stock of products, clear reporting, purchase authorisations and integrated purchase requisitions. This tracking of the entire process facilitates audit checks or in any other future verifications.

Financial

This is an independent financial management suite that assists in easy handling of the finances of your organisation. Some of the key features are balance sheets, profit & loss ledgers, easy collections, fee reminders.

SMS & E-mail

Any information regarding the institution or the student can be sent via SMS and email notifications to the respective individual/s using this portal.

Online Diary

To ensure transparency among students, teachers, parents and the management, this portal helps you to update academic performances, remarks, attendance and a complete overview of the student.

Library

This helps librarians in a detailed tracking from managing the stock to allotment of books to students. Students can research and reserve books in prior as well using this portal.

Election

Election management lets you to conduct your institution's election accurately. Some of the key features involved in this are nominations, multiple election posts, multiple votes for users, secured online voting, publishing of election results.

Security
