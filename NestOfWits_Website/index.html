Vision

Features

.

Student Profile

Displays the complete profile of a student with automated comments and the smooth progression of a student.

Online Classes

Teachers can now schedule and take classes online at any time from anywhere.

Daily Classes

You can now upload the classes in a video format for the students to view anytime and from anywhere.

Library

This helps librarians in a detailed tracking from managing the stock to allotment of books to students. Students can research and reserve books in prior as well using this portal.

Assignments & Tests

Assignments and tests can be updated for every subject by the teachers along with descriptions and submission dates for the students to view on the web browser or mobile app.

Notes & Presentations

You can now update the students
with all the necessary notes and presentations for every subject in the most efficient and organized
structure.

Attendance Management

Lets you track attendance of students and staff subject wise or day wise. Attendance can be calculated by using devices such as RFID/Biometric. You can auto calculate attendance date, generate auto alerts for regular absentees, and share the data with students and parents.

Circulars and Notices

Any information related to your organization such as events,
cultural, competitions, information
to parents, holidays can be
published in this portal for parents, students, and staff to view anytime and can also be downloaded as an attachment.

Parent/Student Portal

A customized sign-in for parents and students provides transparent information about academic activities. Notification and updates will be sent to parents on their child’s progress, students can access their assignments and participate in various forums. This can be viewed online from any location.
